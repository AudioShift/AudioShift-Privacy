# AudioShift Privacy Policy

Privacy policy for the AudioShift app.

## Privacy Policy

AudioShift processes audio files selected by the user and may access the microphone if the user chooses to record audio.

## Data Collection

AudioShift does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to the developer or third parties.

## Local Files

The app accesses audio files and folders selected by the user in order to play and analyze local audio.

## Microphone

If you choose to record audio, the app requests microphone access. Recordings are stored locally on your device.

## Third Parties

AudioShift does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external data collection services.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact: Audio1Shift@gmail.com
